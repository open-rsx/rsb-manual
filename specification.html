Transports¶

There are two kinds of transport implementations in RSB:

  1. Transport implementations which use a RSB-specific communication protocol. These can be employed in RSB-only systems and selected based on their functional and non-functional properties. The following transport implementations fall into this category:

    • Inprocess Transport
    • Spread Transport
    • TCP-Socket Transport

  2. Transport implementations allowing RSB clients to communicate with components which are based on other middlewares. This category consists of the following transport implementations:

    • YARP Transports
    • ROS Transports
